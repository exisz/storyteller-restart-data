# 02 Story Spine — From Exit to Witness

1. The audit opens a glass gate, but the city beyond it has no living pedestrians, only approved exits. Truth shift: escape is an administrative status, not a location. Pressure: a turn-14 event forces who answers the gate first.

2. Linxi reaches the glass where the Seventh Sunrise has been knocking. Truth shift: the Seventh Sunrise is a previous Linxi who refused to sign, not merely a target. Pressure: bringing her out makes her visible; leaving her hidden makes trust fragile.

3. The next city demands household registration. Truth shift: the system can import people only as records. Pressure: forge Linxi as a resident or register the Seventh Sunrise as a living debt.

4. The broadcast tower detects the contradiction. Truth shift: the radio has been relaying not only warnings but audit queries. Pressure: expose the operator or trade the map for a controlled lie.

# 01 Architecture — Ward Zero public living-discharge hearing

Act/region: Ward Zero hearing chamber after the living-discharge bell.

Main pressure: the hospital must hold a hearing before killing the claimant, but it will try to define the audience, evidence, and verdict as corpse paperwork.

Side pressures:
- Seventh Sunrise must stay named as a living claimant, not converted back into inventory.
- Linxi's B-07/0 contradiction can be used as witness evidence or sacrificed as a decoy.
- Yue's earlier debt can be converted into testimony, but doing so creates hospital leverage.
- The broadcast/operator voice can falsify minutes unless pinned to stamped evidence.

Player choices:
- public docket vs controlled clerk docket;
- oath anchored in Seventh Sunrise's pulse vs Linxi/B-07/0 paper contradiction;
- rebut hospital objections with supply-chain evidence vs force Yue to testify;
- lock the hearing minutes publicly vs smuggle a verdict through corpse appeal;
- accept a provisional living-discharge writ vs redirect discharge protection to Linxi.

Topology: five sequential rites with two condition-triggered pressure events. Events use `trigger: {condition: true}` with `NOT` answered guards, never pure turn gates. Branch flags merge for playability but are remembered for later discharge/appeal consequences.

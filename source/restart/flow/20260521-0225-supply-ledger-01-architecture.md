# Architecture — Supply Ledger Account House

Region: supply-ledger account house, an administrative layer between paper factory, broadcast tower, and old Sunrise Hospital.

Main pressure: the account house does not print deaths; it allocates how many people are allowed to be printed as dead. Linxi must turn worker testimony and stolen mold evidence into control over the quota ledger before the next hospital shipment leaves.

Side pressures:
- proof vs sabotage: publicly audit the ledger with worker testimony, or secretly mark one shipment as living paper;
- witness safety: protecting workers and the Seventh Sunrise makes stronger evidence but creates names for the clerks to target;
- broadcast debt: tower/operator tools can open doors, but using them teaches the system where Linxi is.

Topology expectation: rite 26 enters from `restart-foreman-supply-ledger-coordinate-decoded`. Event 27 creates an audit/sabotage choice remembered by rite 27/28. Rite 28 grants a quota token. Event 29 pressures how to spend the token. Rite 29 converts it into a living-paper lot. Rite 30 opens the hospital-return invoice route for the next packet.

Delayed consequences: flags distinguish testimony audit vs silent shelf-mark, worker witness protection vs shipment contamination, quota freeze vs redirected quota, and whether the next route is an open indictment or a covert hospital return.

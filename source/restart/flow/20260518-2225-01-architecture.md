# 01 Architecture — eighth-loop signature arc

Region: ambulance platform → no-exit tunnel → underground signature room.

Main pressure: the player cannot simply board the ambulance; the system demands a trade between who keeps memory, who keeps hospital access, and whose death can be turned into an exit document.

Side pressures:
- Yue can still help, but preserving his access may make him more able to shift his debt onto Linxi.
- The radio/broadcast tries to erase or rewrite the real bed number.
- Paperwork remains causal: a death certificate can create the exit rather than merely record death.

Topology: rite 09 triggers event 10; event 10 opens rite 10. Rite 10 grants wristband/ampoule state and triggers event 11. Event 11 opens rite 11, which grants real bed number and blank death certificate, then triggers event 12. Event 12 opens rite 12 as the next branch point.

Delayed consequences: flags remember whether Linxi protected memory, preserved Yue access, memorized/stamped the bed number, and chose rescue vs debt truth. Later content can check these flags to decide who can leave and who is treated as officially dead.

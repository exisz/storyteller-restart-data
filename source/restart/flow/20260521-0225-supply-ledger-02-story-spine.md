# Story Spine — Supply Ledger Account House

1. Linxi follows the decoded coordinate to a ledger room where blank death-certificate paper is counted before names exist. Truth: death is rationed before it is diagnosed. Emotional shift: the enemy becomes banal and logistical. Pressure: choose how to enter the books.
2. A quota bell announces that old Sunrise Hospital is short one corpse. Truth: the system can demand a death to balance paper stock. Pressure: protect witnesses or poison the outgoing lot.
3. The allocation clerks reveal that every rescued living person creates a quota deficit. Truth: rescue has an accounting shadow. Pressure: freeze the quota openly or redirect it covertly.
4. The living paper lot proves a batch can be shipped back to hospital without accepting death-watermark authority. Truth: the supply chain can be reversed. Pressure: decide whether to carry proof to hospital or turn it into a moving sanctuary.
5. The return invoice opens the next route: not escape from hospital, but returning with control over its paper supply. Truth: Linxi can attack the source of official death from inside the hospital mechanism.

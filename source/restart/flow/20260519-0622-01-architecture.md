# 01 Architecture — City Beyond the Audit

Act/region: Act Two threshold, immediately after the false-exit audit opens the old station gate.

Main pressure: the exit is conditional; the city beyond the gate accepts paperwork before it accepts living people. Linxi must decide whether the Seventh Sunrise becomes a rescued witness, a hidden guide, or another forged identity.

Side pressures:
- The broadcast follows the missing name and tries to reattach it to Linxi.
- Yue's testimony is useful but politically dangerous in the next region.
- The audit stamp can open doors, but it also marks the bearer as a document anomaly.
- The radio is no longer passive; it can answer the city and attract the broadcast tower.

Topology: `restart-rite-13-false-exit-audit` feeds a turn-14 event and a new Act Two rite. The rite grants concrete state cards (`第七次日出`, `假出口审计章`), then a registry rite grants a map, then the broadcast tower turns those resources into the next conflict. Branch flags are stored for later Act Two consequences.

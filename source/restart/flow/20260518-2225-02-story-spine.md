# 02 Story Spine — eighth-loop signature arc

1. The ambulance becomes a transfer vehicle rather than an escape. Truth: the next loop is reachable, but only one form of continuity can travel safely. Emotional shift: hope becomes triage. Gameplay pressure: memory vs access.
2. The eighth-loop wristband proves the future Linxi may be real, but the proof is also hospital paperwork. Truth: even proof can be manufactured. Emotional shift: suspicion returns. Gameplay pressure: stabilize proof before broadcast erases it.
3. The real bed number is B-07/0, a signature room rather than a ward. Truth: Seventh Sunrise is tied to refusal to sign, not merely being hidden. Emotional shift: the mystery becomes moral complicity. Gameplay pressure: choose who testifies and who may pay.
4. The blank death certificate makes exit possible only through an official lie. Truth: escape requires creating a death the system will accept. Emotional shift: agency becomes dangerous. Gameplay pressure: sign Linxi's decoy death or Yue's debt death later.

# 02 Story Spine — Ward Zero living-discharge execution

1. **Ward Zero gate**
   - Situation: the living discharge preorder reaches a door marked for corpses only.
   - Truth/change: a living order only matters if it can pass dead infrastructure.
   - Emotional shift: hope becomes procedural danger.
   - Gameplay pressure: choose whose name bears the discharge contradiction.

2. **Pulse arrears**
   - Situation: the corridor detects an unpaid pulse.
   - Truth/change: life itself is now a debt line.
   - Emotional shift: being alive becomes incriminating.
   - Gameplay pressure: protect witnesses/evidence or let Linxi carry the pulse debt.

3. **Elevator inspection**
   - Situation: the corpse elevator rejects living weight.
   - Truth/change: the hospital has secretly transported living patients as cargo before.
   - Emotional shift: the machinery becomes a witness.
   - Gameplay pressure: expose or bribe the elevator system.

4. **Downcall and intake clerk**
   - Situation: Ward Zero's intake clerk tries to file the living passenger as late corpse stock.
   - Truth/change: discharge and death appeal share the same counter.
   - Emotional shift: bureaucracy is not faceless; someone stamps the lie.
   - Gameplay pressure: bind an unreturned pulse or force the archive location.

5. **Pre-death roll call**
   - Situation: the ward recites names that should arrive dead.
   - Truth/change: the system pre-attends its victims.
   - Emotional shift: the player hears their future being called.
   - Gameplay pressure: hide Seventh Sunrise or broadcast Linxi's delayed-corpse contradiction.

6. **Discharge hearing bell**
   - Situation: a not-yet-dead stamp can summon a hearing.
   - Truth/change: Ward Zero can be made to hold court before it kills.
   - Emotional shift: escape becomes indictment.
   - Gameplay pressure: choose public hearing route or corpse-appeal backdoor.

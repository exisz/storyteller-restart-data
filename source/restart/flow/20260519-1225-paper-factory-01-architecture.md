# Architecture — Death-Paper Factory

Region: Paper Factory, the supply chain behind Sunrise Hospital's death certificates.

Main pressure: the factory can print death into paperwork only while it controls blank paper, watermark molds, and night-shift witnesses. The Player must decide whether to contaminate the paper supply, preserve live testimony, or bargain with the factory foreman.

Side pressures:
- Seventh Sunrise as protected living witness vs expendable proof.
- Tower Operator as debtor/guide whose voice can spoof factory announcements.
- Linxi's living-file copy as a future self-proof that may cost paper supply access.

Choices needed:
- follow witness route or operator-debt route into the factory;
- steal a watermark mold or contaminate blank certificate paper;
- protect workers as witnesses or expose the foreman as another paperwork agent.

Delayed memory:
- night-exit route choice changes who is trusted at the factory gate;
- paper contamination vs watermark theft changes later certificate authority;
- worker testimony vs foreman bargain changes next-region ally/lock.

Topology: rite 21 opens from `restart-night-exit-ledger-filed`, event 22 taxes the chosen route, rite 22 grants factory proof cards, rite 23 turns proof into a next-region branch.

# Branch Design — Death-Paper Factory

Rite 21: Paper-factory tram. Requires night ledger opened plus living-file/city-map/seventh-sun. Grants `纸厂夜班票` and opens route flag based on choice: protect witness route or operator debt route.

Event 22: Night-shift paper tax. Player pays with audit-stamp cover or operator voice. Flags record whether the paper route is shielded or spoofed.

Rite 22: Watermark mold room. Requires paper tax paid, night ticket, audit stamp/living file. Grants `死亡证明水印模` and `未裁切证明纸`. Choice steals mold for future anti-forgery or contaminates paper supply to protect unnamed live files.

Rite 23: Foreman witness ledger. Requires mold room opened plus one proof card. Grants `纸厂工人证词` and opens next-region branch. Choice protects worker witnesses or bargains with foreman for supply-chain coordinates.

Costs/risks: using the operator makes a stronger shortcut but deepens broadcast debt; protecting Seventh Sunrise makes better testimony but keeps the witness in danger. Paper contamination helps many, mold theft gives precise proof.

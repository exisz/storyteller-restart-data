# Story Spine — Death-Paper Factory

1. The night exit does not lead to a street; it leads to a tram that delivers blank paper to the old hospital. Truth: deaths are not merely certified after the fact; the paper is prepared to accept certain names.

2. The factory gate recognizes either the Seventh Sunrise's protected witness route or the Tower Operator's debt route. Emotional shift: the rescued people are not just companions, they are credentials with costs.

3. The watermark room reveals that every death certificate has a hidden mold. Pressure: stealing the mold prevents future forged deaths, but contaminating the paper supply can protect more unnamed live people immediately.

4. A night-shift audit forces Linxi to decide whose testimony leaves the factory. Pressure: workers can become witnesses, or the foreman can be forced into a bargain that opens the next place in the supply chain.

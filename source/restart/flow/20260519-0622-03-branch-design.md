# 03 Branch Design — Act Two Threshold

Playable choices:
- Turn-14 event: follow the three knocks from the Seventh Sunrise, or let the radio answer the audit query. This creates `restart_followed_seventh_knock` or `restart_radio_answered_exit_audit`.
- Rite 14: break the audit glass and rescue the witness, or keep her behind glass as a remote guide. Both contact the Seventh Sunrise but create different risk flags.
- Rite 15: forge Linxi into the city registry, or register the Seventh Sunrise as a living debt. This turns the audit stamp into either cover or obligation.
- Turn-15 event: hide the missing name in static, or let the broadcast tower hear it. This sets up the tower.
- Rite 16/17: decide whether the broadcast operator becomes an exposed enemy, a bargained-with tool, or a source of a living file.

Cards used/created: `第七次日出` (person/witness), `假出口审计章` (item/access/risk), `伪城地图` (item/location/route), `第一份活人档案` (item/proof).

Delayed consequences: witness visibility, radio complicity, forged citizenship, living debt, and operator bargain flags are intentionally recorded for Act Two.

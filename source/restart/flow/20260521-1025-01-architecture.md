# 01 Architecture — Hospital supply reversal packet

## Act / region

Return to old Sunrise Hospital, via the supply receiving dock beneath Ward Seven.

## Main pressure

The hospital cannot deny a sealed supply return, but it can reclassify the living-paper lot as ordinary death-certificate stock unless Linxi ties it to a living witness, the supply ledger, and the original B-07/0 intake record.

## Side pressures

- Public indictment vs covert re-entry: current save has public account route, but player choices still decide whether to force public intake or preserve a quiet route.
- Seventh Sunrise as protected witness vs evidence-bearer at risk of being warehoused.
- Tower operator must either broadcast a contradiction or become a hospital debtor.
- Linxi can use B-07/0 to reverse her own pre-death record, but that exposes the ward-zero shelf.

## Player choices that should exist

1. Declare the shipment at the receiving dock or mislabel it as ordinary return stock.
2. During intake siren, protect Seventh Sunrise's living name or expose the operator's false intake voice.
3. Audit the returned paper as evidence or use it as a trap for the hospital's intake clerk.
4. Confront the B-07/0 shelf by filing Linxi's bed number or forcing the hospital to count its missing corpse.
5. Convert the returned batch into a discharge order or a ward-zero summons.

## Required future memory

- Whether the hospital return was public or hidden.
- Whether Seventh Sunrise is the named receiving witness.
- Whether the operator was bound to hospital intake.
- Whether B-07/0 was reversed as a live bed or used to expose the missing-corpse count.

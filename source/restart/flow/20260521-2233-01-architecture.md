# 01 Architecture — Ward Zero living-discharge execution

## Region

Ward Zero, the hospital layer that normally only processes corpses and already-signed deaths.

## Main pressure

A living discharge preorder is not an exit. It must be executed through a department whose doors, elevators, clerks, and hearing bells only recognize dead patients. The player must make the hospital count a living person without letting it convert that person into a corpse for bookkeeping.

## Side pressures

- Seventh Sunrise can be protected as the public living witness, but that exposes her name to Ward Zero.
- Linxi can absorb the contradiction through B-07/0, but that makes her the hospital's reusable delayed corpse.
- Existing supply-chain evidence can pay costs, but using it may expose worker testimony or the living-paper lot.

## Player choices

- Publish Seventh Sunrise's discharge route vs route it through Linxi's B-07/0 summons.
- Pay pulse arrears with worker/supply evidence vs let Linxi's pulse carry the debt.
- Force machinery to admit it moves living people vs bribe/redirect inspection with hospital's own seals.
- Create a public hearing case vs slip into a corpse-appeal backdoor.

## Required future memory

The packet should leave branch flags for later: public Seventh discharge, Linxi delayed-corpse route, pulse-debt placement, elevator confession/bribe, hearing corridor vs corpse appeal.

## Graph topology

Linear main spine for five turns with two turn-triggered pressure events. Branches merge into the next route but keep memory via flags/cards. Cards created here are resources for future Ward Zero hearing content.

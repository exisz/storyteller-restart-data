# Architecture — discharged public witness escort

Region: the station-like transfer zone outside Ward Zero, immediately after Seventh Sunrise signs the exit ledger.

Main pressure: the hospital accepts the discharge as public record but treats every public witness as an addressable pursuit target. The Player must convert a discharged witness ledger into an escort/protection route before the pursuit number can attach to Seventh Sunrise or Linxi.

Side pressures:
- Publicness protects the ruling but exposes the gallery.
- Linxi can carry B-07/0 pursuit debt as a decoy, but doing so gives the system a later way to reopen her bed slot.
- The radio/operator can jam pursuit announcements at a cost.

Playable choices:
- register the escort under public witness law vs hide it under Linxi's bed-zero shelter;
- spend public gallery / hearing records vs use broadcast/operator contradictions;
- cut off the pursuit number vs convert it into evidence for the next route.

Topology: enter from `restart_next_region_public_witness_escort_route` or the alternative bed-zero pursuit flag from rite 50. Create a six-rite chain with two condition-driven events. Events use `trigger: {condition: true}` and flags/cards, with `NOT:*_answered` guards.

Future memory: public escort vs bed-zero decoy, gallery-exposed vs operator-jammed pursuit, protected witness route vs Linxi pursuit route.

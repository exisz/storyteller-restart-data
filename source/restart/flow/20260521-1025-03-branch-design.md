# 03 Branch Design — Hospital supply reversal

## Cards

- `医院收货铅封`: proof the returned tram entered hospital logistics.
- `收货口活人签收单`: a receiving form that can name a living person as consignee.
- `倒扣病床账页`: ledger page showing bed B-07/0 was charged before a patient arrived.
- `活人出院预令`: discharge paperwork that can later be spent to get a living person past hospital authority.

## Rites / events

- Rite 31 `旧日出收货月台`: consumes sealed return tram state, return invoice, living paper, and supply ledger; grants dock seal and living receipt.
- Event 32 `收货口活体警报`: chooses protecting Seventh Sunrise's receiver name vs binding the operator to intake contradiction.
- Rite 32 `回院验货审计`: uses return invoice / living paper / dock seal after event answer; grants reverse bed ledger.
- Rite 33 `B-07/0倒扣病床`: uses true bed number and reverse ledger to decide whether Linxi's bed becomes live or the hospital is forced to count the missing corpse.
- Event 34 `病人库存盘点`: forces a cost around worker testimony vs living paper batch.
- Rite 34 `病历反装订架`: binds the patient-stock result into the living file and worker testimony, opening discharge authority.
- Rite 35 `活人出院预令`: grants the next region exit/summons state and creates a durable discharge-order card.

## Costs and risks

Costs are expressed as branch flags and favor/influence shifts. Public choices gain influence and make the hospital acknowledge contradiction; quieter or deceptive choices gain favor but leave later debt hooks. Events deliberately introduce risk flags consumed by later rites so they are not isolated flavor.

## Delayed consequences

- `restart_seventh_sun_named_as_hospital_receiver` vs `restart_operator_bound_to_hospital_intake_voice`.
- `restart_linxi_bed_zero_reversed_as_live` vs `restart_hospital_missing_corpse_count_forced`.
- `restart_patient_stock_count_protected_worker_names` vs `restart_patient_stock_count_marked_living_batch`.
- Packet exit: `restart_living_discharge_order_issued` and either `restart_next_region_seventh_sun_discharge_route` or `restart_next_region_ward_zero_summons_route`.

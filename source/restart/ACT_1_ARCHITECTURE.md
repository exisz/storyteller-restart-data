# RESTART Act 1 Architecture

## Act Purpose

Act 1 teaches the player that the route is repeating, that official instructions are unreliable, and that Sunrise Hospital is not a place of treatment but a mechanism for assigning deaths.

By the end of Act 1, the player should understand:

- the broadcast guides behavior but may be protecting or harvesting Linxi;
- records can exist before events happen;
- Yue doctor is lying, but the lie may be a survival tactic;
- Ward Seven is the first visible layer of the system;
- escape requires manipulating documents, memory, and trust, not just finding an exit.

## Regions

### Station

Role: establish loop, broadcast authority, and initial uncertainty.

Pressures:
- blend in or stand out;
- trust broadcast or test it;
- preserve memory or trade safety for speed.

### Train

Role: transition from public route to controlled route; introduce memory gaps and passengers as witnesses/suspects.

Pressures:
- open the black box or keep it hidden;
- take medicine or resist it;
- follow passenger clues or protect current objective.

### Sunrise Hospital

Role: reveal medical/paperwork authority and force Linxi into document-based danger.

Pressures:
- Yue doctor as liar/helper/hostage;
- case records vs lived memory;
- death certificates as both clue and weapon.

### Ward Seven

Role: first direct confrontation with the system’s rules.

Pressures:
- choose which death record to falsify, destroy, or fulfill;
- decide whether Yue is expendable;
- discover what the Seventh Sunrise wants from Linxi.

## Active Threads

- Main spine: station → train → hospital → ward seven → first exit attempt.
- Broadcast thread: instruction, manipulation, contradiction, possible sentience/operator.
- Yue thread: lie, leverage, debt, possible betrayal/protection.
- Linxi identity thread: conflicting death records and memory discontinuity.
- Paperwork thread: records can cause reality, not merely describe it.

## Required Branch Quality

Each region should contain optional pressure that can change a later region. For example:

- trusting broadcast at Station changes how Hospital announcements address Linxi;
- protecting Yue changes access inside Ward Seven;
- resisting medicine changes memory but increases immediate cost;
- stealing or altering paperwork creates a future contradiction the system reacts to.

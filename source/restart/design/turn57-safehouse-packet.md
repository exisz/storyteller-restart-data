# Turn 57 protected-witness safehouse packet

## Architecture
The previous packet got Seventh Sunrise into a witness safehouse, but a safehouse cannot be a passive shelter: hospital authority follows through curfew reports, signal tracing, and address-based pursuit. This packet turns the safehouse into a public testimony relay that can move evidence beyond the hospital boundary.

## Story spine
1. Register the protected witness and split person / docket / pursuit number.
2. Lock public names into a safehouse logbook, creating a new pressure surface.
3. Answer the curfew knock and reverse the curfew warrant.
4. Use the roof antenna to broadcast living testimony outside hospital jurisdiction.
5. Break signal triangulation and convert the relay into signed testimony.
6. Hand the affidavit across the dawn border, opening the next outside-witness route.

## Branch design
Public route flags keep Seventh Sunrise as the named protected witness; favor/decoy route flags let Linxi shelter or redirect parts of the pursuit. Events are condition-triggered and guarded so late-game pressure is state-driven. New cards are used as playable state: custodian, lockbook, borrowed pulse lantern, curfew warrant, antenna key, relay sheet, and dawn affidavit.

Adds rites 57-62, events 58 and 60, and seven cards. The next Player should be able to advance through at least five turns from canonical turn 57.
